Building directly alongside this, the `BacktestMetrics` schema definitively defines the exact structural blueprint of the fully completed algorithmic simulation, precisely ready for incredibly complex performance review. By explicitly and rigorously defining this complex schema, we systematically establish an incredibly clear, unbreakable contract strictly between the complex backtesting layer (acting as the producer) and the final reporting or visualization layers (acting as the critical consumers). The consumers can rely with absolute mathematical certainty on the fact that any incredibly complex metrics they receive have definitively passed this extraordinarily strict validation process.

The fundamental operation of the entire analytical system absolutely relies incredibly heavily on these precise Pydantic schemas.
